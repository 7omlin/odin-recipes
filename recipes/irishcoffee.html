Irish Coffee

Description

Ingredients

  • 0.5oz - Jameson Irish Whiskey
  • 0.5oz - Bailey's Original Irish Cream Liqueur
  • 5oz - Coffee
  • 0.25oz - Creme de Menthe Liqueur
  • Whipped Cream

Steps

  1. To a clear glass mug add Jameson and Bailey's.
  2. Fill mug rest of the way with coffee.
  3. Top with whipped cream. Drizzle green creme de menthe onto cream topper.
