Keoke Coffee

Description

Ingredients

  • 0.5oz - Jameson Irish Whiskey
  • 0.25oz - Kahlua Coffee Liqueur
  • 0.25oz - Creme de Cacao Liqueur
  • 5oz - Coffee
  • Whipped Cream

Steps

  1. To a clear glass mug add Jameson, Kahlua and Creme de Cacao.
  2. Fill mug rest of the way with coffee.
  3. Top with whipped cream.
