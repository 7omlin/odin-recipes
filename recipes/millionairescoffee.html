Millionaire's Coffee

Description

Ingredients

  • 0.5oz - Bailey's Original Irish Cream Liqueur
  • 0.5oz - Grand Marnier Orange Liqueur
  • 0.25oz - Kahlua Coffee Liqueur
  • 0.25oz - Frangelico Hazelnut Liqueur
  • 5oz - Coffee
  • Whipped Cream

Steps

  1. To a clear glass mug add Bailey's, Grand Manier, Frangelico and Kahlua.
  2. Fill mug rest of the way with coffee.
  3. Top with whipped cream.
